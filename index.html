Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register

What's included in my ticket? +

Every ticket includes access to all keynotes and talks, workshop sessions based on your tier, networking lunch and coffee breaks, a conference swag bag, and digital recordings for 30 days after the event.

Can I attend virtually? +

DevConf 2026 is an in-person event, but all sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees for 30 days after the conference ends.

What is your refund policy? +

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Within 30 days, tickets can be transferred to a colleague at no extra cost, but refunds are not guaranteed.

Are there dietary accommodations? +

Yes, vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and halal meal options are available. Please select your preference during registration so catering can prepare accordingly.

Where can I find the venue address and travel info? +

Venue details, nearby hotel recommendations, and parking/transit information will be emailed to all registered attendees two weeks before the event.
